My favorite movies are Transformers and Batman(Darknight Trilogy). Despite the fact that I'm not a big fan of drama, I love the film Hidden Figures directed by Theodore Melfi. My hobbies are cycling, studying, listening to music and travelling. Another important information about me, I am a cat lover. Meet them on Pets. I have had a dream of becoming an engineer since I was a kid . I have ended up being a teacher because of my family's social and financial conditions. Studying Engineering in Brasil is very expensive. Then I decided to take another path and start studying English. Since I was an excellent student, only 3 years after I had started attending the English classes, I was invited by the English school I used to study in, to particiate in a training course and then become a teacher. I fell in love with teaching. You may think "why are you attending CS50 classes?". CS50 is the fisrt step for me to fulfill my childhood dream to be an engineer.
