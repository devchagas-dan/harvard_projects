My cats!

Tom

Tom is a straight cat who is 15 years old. He's lovely, gentle and needy. He loves affection and companion.

Nina

Nina is half Persian half straight cat who is 4 years old. She is such a lady, very quiet, calm. She has an anti social behaviour...hehehe. She is not needy, doesn't like affection.

Simba

Simba is my beloved Frajolinha (How we Brazilians name most black and white cats). He is wonderful! Such a lovely cat. He loves interacting with humans, in general. He is only 3 years old.

Fumacinha

Fumacinha(Smoky) is a very energetic cat, I think because he is a kitten yet. He's got his name because of his smoky face. He bothers everyone in the house.

Lynx

Lynx is Fumacinha's sister. I've rescued them from street. They were abandoned by their previous human in a weed near my mom's house. Lynx has got her name because she looks like a real Lynx but smaller. She is very energetic too, but very affectionate!

I love them!! They're all family members
